Our Services

Utilizing state-of-the-art equipment to deliver exceptional results.

Rotary Rig Piling

Our powerful rotary rigs are ideal for boring large diameter piles in a wide range of soil and rock conditions. This method is highly efficient and allows for deep and robust foundation construction, making it suitable for major infrastructure and high-rise building projects.

Tripod Rig Piling

Tripod rigs are the workhorse for sites with restricted access or challenging ground conditions. Their smaller footprint and versatile setup allow for precise piling work in confined spaces. This method is perfect for residential projects, underpinning, and sites where minimal disruption is key.

DTH (Down-the-Hole) Rig Piling

When it comes to drilling through hard rock and dense formations, our DTH rigs are the solution. Using a percussive hammer mechanism, this method provides rapid and effective drilling for rock-socketed piles, ensuring a solid foundation even in the most demanding geological environments.
